## How to Join

We welcome developers, dreamers, and doers who believe technology should serve a higher purpose.

**Steps to become a contributor:**

1. **Review our mission** - Understand what we build and why
2. **Explore the code** - Familiarize yourself with our projects
3. **Start contributing** - Submit your first pull request, issue, or suggestion
4. **Engage with the community** - Join discussions, attend meetings, collaborate
5. **Become a maintainer** - As you deepen involvement, earn responsibility for projects

**We value:**
- Humility and respect
- Genuine desire to help
- Collaborative spirit
- Accountability to purpose
- Long-term commitment

## Decision Making

**What the director does:**
- Guide overall vision
- Coordinate between projects
- Remove blockers
- Facilitate discussions
- Serve the community's needs

**What the community decides:**
- Project direction and priorities
- Major feature additions
- Code of conduct enforcement
- Contributor recognition
- Resource allocation

**Decision types:**

| Decision | Process |
|----------|---------|
| Day-to-day code decisions | Individual maintainers + code review |
| Project priorities | Team discussion + consensus |
| Major direction changes | Community vote (48 hours) |
| Code of conduct violations | Investigation + director decision |
| Governance changes | Full community vote (7 days) |

---

## Decision Making

**During Foundation Startup Phase:**
Until the community establishes a formal decision-making process, the director makes decisions unilaterally to keep momentum and move the foundation forward. This is temporary and necessary for initial growth.

**Long-term (Once Process is Established):**
Once we have active contributors and stable projects, we will transition to community-driven decision-making outlined below.

---

**What the director does (during startup):**
- Make urgent decisions
- Guide overall vision

- Remove blockers

## Code of Conduct

**Our guiding principle:** Work together with humility, recognizing that our abilities are gifts.

**We commit to:**
- Building with unity, not for our own glory
- Serving others above ourselves
- Remaining accountable to a purpose higher than ourselves
- Honoring our work and benefiting humanity

**We will not tolerate:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or abuse
- Ego-driven decisions that harm the community
- Misuse of resources or credit
- Exclusion of voices or perspectives
- Violation of trust or transparency

**Conflict resolution:**
1. Direct conversation between parties
2. Mediation by a neutral community member
3. Director involvement if unresolved
4. Community review for serious violations

## Project Roles

**Director**
- Stewards the overall vision
- Facilitates community decisions
- Handles final escalations
- Maintains transparency
- Accountable to the community

**Project Maintainers**
- Own a specific project's quality
- Review and merge code
- Triage issues and discussions
- Mentor new contributors
- Report progress to community

**Contributors**
- Submit code, ideas, or feedback
- Follow code of conduct
- Participate in discussions
- Help review others' work
- Give feedback to maintainers

**Users**
- Use the software
- Report bugs
- Request features
- Share feedback
- Contribute improvements

## Amendments to This Document

**How to propose changes:**
1. Create a GitHub discussion or issue
2. Explain your proposal clearly
3. Allow 1 week for community feedback
4. Hold a vote if significant

**Voting threshold for governance changes:**
- 66% majority required (higher bar)
- 7-day voting period
- All contributors eligible to vote
- Decision is binding

**Review cycle:**
- This document reviewed annually
- Emergency amendments allowed with 80% vote
